General Guidelines

1. Only registered participants will be allowed to attend the event , on spot registrations will not be entertained.

2. It is mandatory for the participants to provide their college ID at the help desk for verification.

3. Proper attire should be followed by the participants.

4. A participant is not restricted to participation in any number of events as long as the event timings do not clash.

5. All the events will commence at the scheduled time , participants are requested to be punctual.

6. Participants are requested to collect the event tags from the help desk present in the workshop block .

7. No refund on the registration fees will be encouraged.

8. Students should refrain from abusive language, obscene display and a good decorum will be appreciated.

9. Smoking, consumption of alcohol or any such substance is strictly prohibited inside the college premises.

10. Rules are subject to changes at the discretion of the management or the event organisers.
